FAQ

1. Why register here?

Registering here is the quickest and easiest solution for documenting new and existing employees. It ensures that all necessary information is securely collected and organized for HR purposes, streamlining the onboarding process and ensuring accurate record-keeping.

2. How to register?

To register, users simply need to click on the "New Registration" tab at the top of the page and fill out the Employee Details form. The form is straightforward and easy to complete, requiring basic information about the employee's personal and contact details, previous work experience, qualifications, and more.

3. Can I complete registration on my phone?

Yes! Registration can be completed on any device with internet access, including your phone. Simply copy and paste the URL into your mobile browser, and you can fill out the form anytime, anywhere. This flexibility allows for convenient registration, whether you're at home, in the office, or on the go.
